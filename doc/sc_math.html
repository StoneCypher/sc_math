gcd/2

gcd(A, B) -> any()

Produces the greatest common divisor of two integers.

Why is by-zero undefined? Because gcd divides both numbers then gcd must not be larger than either number. Because gcd is the denominator, then if either number is zero, the denominator must not exceed zero, and therefore must be zero. Division by zero is undefined. Therefore gcd(0,_) or gcd(_,0) must be undefined.
